I am Liyuan Pan, a Research Professor and PhD Supervisor at the School of Computer Science, Beijing Institute of Technology, as well as a recipient of the National Young Talents Program.

I got my PhD degree from the Australian National University in 2021, supervised by Dr. Richard Hartley, Miaomiao Liu, Yuchao Dai and Hongdong Li. From 2021 to 2022, I worked as a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the Australian National University under the supervision of Dr. Eric Stone. I joined the School of Computer Science, Beijing Institute of Technology, in June 2022.

I have published more than 30 first-author or corresponding-author papers in top-tier international conferences and journals on computer vision and machine learning, including ICCV, CVPR, TPAMI and NeurIPS. I ranked 3rd worldwide in the Google Landmark Retrieval Challenge and received the Outstanding Reviewer Award at ICCV 2021. My primary research interests cover event cameras, dual-pixel sensors, scene understanding, low-level vision, VLM, and biomedical image processing.
